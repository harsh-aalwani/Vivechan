The Problem

Manual evaluation is
broken at scale.

Universities evaluate millions of answer sheets every semester. The traditional process is inefficient, inconsistent, and unfair to students.

⏰

Time-Intensive Process

Teachers spend hours per exam checking papers manually — leaving little time for teaching and research.

⚖️

Inconsistent Marking

Same answer, different scores. Marking varies across evaluators creating unfair outcomes for students.

📅

Processing Delays

Results take days or weeks. Students wait without knowing where they lost marks or how to improve.

📝

Limited Feedback

Students receive only a final score — no understanding of mistakes or guidance on improvement.

📈

Cannot Scale

As institutions grow, manual evaluation becomes a critical bottleneck with no easy solution.
